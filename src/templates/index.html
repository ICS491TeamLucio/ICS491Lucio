This website is created by team Lucio for the HACC of 2017. Even after the HACC 2017 are continuing to update the website. For more information please check our Contact Us page

About this website


This website is a helpful way for the public and government officials to search through different grants and sponsorships. It includes previous historical data that you can compare and see more information about the grant or sponsorship. It also allows users to narrow down searchers to find which grant applies to them.



Please go to the Contact Us page to fill out a survey to improve our website!
